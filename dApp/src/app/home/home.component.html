How It Works

Buddy is a decentralized peer-to-peer crypto marketplace that connects individuals to trade fiat for cryptocurrencies with support for a wide range of payment options, including Bank Transfer, PayPal, and more.

We revolutionize your crypto transactions with our cutting-edge person-to-person smart contract escrow powered crypto-fiat exchange platform. Our innovative solution eliminates middlemen, provides flexibility in payment methods, and ensures trust and security for your transactions.

Benefits of Buddy

Eliminating Middlemen

By facilitating direct transfers between individuals, our platform removes the need for intermediaries such as exchanges or payment processors.

Flexibility in Payment Methods

We support a wide range of fiat payment options, providing users with the freedom to choose the method that suits them best.

Trust and Security

By powering the transfers with smart contract escrows, users can be assured that their funds are protected at all times.
